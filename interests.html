Marathon Running

Having a clear goal and strong plan is essential for any consultant. A great thing about marathon running is that there is a clear goal of 26.2 miles and a proven training schedule. Since I don't have to put any additional thought into the goal or plan, I can focus all my energy on being mentally tough enough to keep up with the 30+ mile weeks and any life challenges that got in the way. Running helps me to realize that the stronger my convictions are in my business goals and in my plan to get there, the more mentally tough I will become.
